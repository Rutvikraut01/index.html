Maharashtra is a land where ancient wonders and natural marvels coexist in perfect harmony. From the awe-inspiring rock-cut temples of Ellora, where entire mountains were sculpted into divine artistry, to the mysterious Lonar Lake, formed by a meteor impact and studied by NASA, this state is a treasure trove of the extraordinary.
